The Best Movies According to Chris

My top 3 movies of all time

The God-father

Amazing soundtrack, setting, acting, plot, everything about this movie makes it one of the greatest films of all time.

Spirited Away

This was my first actual interaction with japanese cinema and animation. I remember crying as a little kid, a wild emotional ride from beginning to end. Fell in love with the world and its characters.

Spotlight

This film opened my eyes to how hypocritical the world can be. It showed me how you cannot accept some views because it is the norm.
